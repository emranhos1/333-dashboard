পূর্বের পাতা

333-a2i has introduced the call centre to easily get data and information from the Bangladesh National Portal from home with the use of mobile phones. After COVID-19, all the citizens of the country can get all the emergency services like relief, doctors service, online product orders, Child marriage information, and social problem remedy, find out more about the information and services by calling 333. 333 Call centre as playing a significant role during the COVID-19 scenario. Almost 75 M+ calls were received from the citizen through 333 for different services.
